Waffles

Ingrediants

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Mix together flour, salt, baking powder, and sugar. Preheat waffle iron.
  2. Beat eggs. Stir in milk, butter, and vanilla. Pour wet mixture into dry mixture, beat until blended.
  3. Ladle batter into waffle iron. Cook until golden.
