Lasagna

Description

A stacked noodle creation.

Ingrediants

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella
  • Chedder Cheese
  • Ricotta Cheese

Steps

  1. Bring pot of salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 350. Brown meat and drain. Stir in sauce and garlic. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Combine Mozzarella, Chedder, and Ricotta. Alternate layers of noodles, meat, and cheese until pan filled.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes.
